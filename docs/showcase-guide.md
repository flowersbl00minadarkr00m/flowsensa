# Flowsensa showcase

1. Open <http://127.0.0.1:4174/> and choose **Explore the demo**.
2. In **Observe**, show human and agent participation, variants, transitions,
   repeats, exceptions, and source evidence.
3. In **Confirm**, add an accountable owner and rationale, then confirm a step.
4. In **Engineer**, compare deterministic/vibe-code, probabilistic/prompt, and
   hybrid/agent-with-harness recommendations. Expand the sixteen factors.
5. In **Ask**, choose repeated work, handoffs, gaps, or treatment suitability.
6. In **Export**, preview JSON, Markdown, and Mermaid.

For the integration story, open canonical FindMnemo at
<http://127.0.0.1:4173/>, create or sync activity, export Telemetry, then import
that JSON into Flowsensa. LocalCFO is demonstrated separately at
<http://127.0.0.1:4175/>.
